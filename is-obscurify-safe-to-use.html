Obscurify is a web application that allows users to analyze their music streaming habits and provides them with insights into their listening preferences.

Obscurify aims to enhance the music streaming experience by offering personalized insights and recommendations based on a user's listening history.

Obscurify's privacy policy outlines the collection of user data including listening history, favorite tracks, and user account information to provide personalized recommendations and insights.

The platform states that it adheres to strict data security measures to protect user information, and it does not sell or share user data with third parties without consent.

While Obscurify claims to prioritize the security and privacy of user data, users should exercise caution when sharing personal information on any online platform.

User feedback on Obscurify has been generally positive, with many users praising the platform for its accurate music recommendations and engaging user interface.

Obscurify integrates with popular music streaming services such as Spotify and Apple Music to access user listening history and provide personalized insights.

While these integrations enhance the user experience, they also raise potential security and privacy concerns.
