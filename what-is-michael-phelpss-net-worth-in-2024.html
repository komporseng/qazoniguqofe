Net Worth Overview

As of 2024, Michael Phelps' estimated net worth stands at an impressive $100 million. This substantial wealth is a result of his extraordinary swimming career, savvy business investments, and lucrative brand endorsements. Despite retiring from competitive swimming, Phelps has continued to thrive through various partnerships with reputable brands, further contributing to his financial prosperity.

Swimming Career and Prize Money

Phelps' dominance in the pool has not only brought him glory but also substantial financial rewards. Throughout his career, he has amassed a total of 28 Olympic medals, including an unprecedented 23 gold medals. This exceptional achievement has earned him substantial prize money amounting to $1.9 million.

Brand Endorsements

Phelps' influence extends beyond the pool, as evidenced by his successful brand endorsements with companies such as AT&T, Speedo, Visa, Subway, Under Armour, and Omega. These partnerships have significantly contributed to his immense wealth, showcasing his value as an iconic figure in the world of sports and beyond.

Entrepreneurial Ventures

In addition to his swimming career and brand endorsements, Phelps has ventured into the world of technology startups, becoming an equity investor in Talkspace, an online mental health startup. This strategic move not only helped break down barriers surrounding mental health but also yielded significant financial rewards, further solidifying his position as a shrewd entrepreneur.
