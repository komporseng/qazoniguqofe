Hazel Gordy: Siblings, Facts About Her Brother &amp; Sisters

Hazel Gordy is grateful to her father. Berry Gordy is one of a number of siblings. Berry has eight children in total, including Hazel. Several of Hazel’s siblings and brothers have followed in their father’s footsteps and worked in the entertainment industry.

They are all gifted in their own right and have found success in the entertainment world. Hazel is the renowned music mogul’s first child. Berry and his first wife, Thelma Coleman, had her and her two biological siblings, Berry Gordy IV and Terry James Gordy.

Hazel gained more siblings when her father Berry married Raynoma Singleton, with whom he had a son, and through his companions Margaret Norton and Jeana Jackson. Berry also has a child with Diana Ross, the I’m Coming Out singer, and another with Nancy Leiviska. The following sections contain information about Hazel’s half-sisters and brothers.

Hazel’s younger brother Kerry Gordy works as a CEO.

Kerry is by far the most well-known of Hazel’s siblings. He entered the music industry as the CEO of Kerry Gordy and Associates Inc. The organization supports well-known musicians in managing their brand partnerships and collecting payments.

Kerry grew up with his father, who helped him find a job in Motown’s mailroom when he was younger. This sibling of Hazel also has a strong background in the music industry. He previously worked for Prince’s record label, Paisley Park Records, as the executive producer of The Jadagrace Show in 2012. He also oversaw the late Rick James’ music career. Kerry is also the spouse and father to Karen Longley and Juliet Gordy.

Another of Hazel Gordy’s siblings, Sherry Gordy, runs a charity.

Berry’s daughter from his relationship with Jeana Jackson is Sherry. Sherry, Hazel’s younger sister, grew up in Southern California and ultimately attended UCLA. In the meantime, she was honing her acting skills through classes. She later earned a real estate license and opened her own firm.

Sherry also leads a humanitarian foundation that she founded in 2004. Her non-profit organization focuses on assisting poor adolescents and young people in improving their educational performance. Sherry also assists them with their job search and emotional well-being.

Kennedy Siblings Hazel Gordy William Gordy, often known as Rockwell, is a well-known singer.

Rockwell, Hazel’s half-brother, is a well-known pop performer. He worked with members of the Jackson 5 to produce his smash single, Somebody’s Watching Me, in 1984.

The song went on to become a huge hit. Between 1984 and 1986, Rockwell released three albums during his career. He also appeared on renowned music shows including American Bandstand and Soul Train.

Rhonda, Hazel’s half-sister

Rhonda is Berry and Diana Ross’s daughter. From 1997 to 1999, she portrayed Toni Burrell in the television series Another World. Rhonda’s mother was Ron Silberstein’s wife at the time of her birth. Rhonda discovered her biological father, Berry, when she was 13 years old.

Rhonda released her debut album, In Case You Didn’t Know, in 2016. In 2017, she also opened for her mother’s show. She has composed two of the songs on Diana’s album earlier. Rodney Kendrick, Rhonda’s husband, is also a musician. They married in 1996, and their first child, Raif Henok Emmanuel Kendrick, was born in 2009.

Another of Hazel Gordy’s siblings is a well-known artist.

Stefan, Hazel’s other sibling, is also an artist. Redfoo is his name. In 2006, he formed the techno duo LMFAO with his nephew Sky Blue. Their debut album, Party Rock, was released in 2009. In 2011, they released Sorry for Party Rocking. Both of the previously mentioned releases were huge successes.

Shots with Lil Jon, Party Rock Anthem, and Sexy and I Know It are some of LMFAO’s most successful tracks. Redfoo also served as a judge on The X Factor Australia as a coach on the US edition from 2013 to 2014. Following that, Redfoo appeared with Emma Slater in the 20th season of Dancing with the Stars.
